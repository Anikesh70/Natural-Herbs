Fenugreek (Methi) Description

1. Fenugreek Hair Mask:
Soak fenugreek seeds overnight in water to soften them.
Grind the soaked seeds into a fine paste the next day.
Optionally, you can add other ingredients such as yogurt, coconut milk, aloe vera gel, or honey to enhance the benefits.
Apply the fenugreek paste to your scalp and hair, focusing on the roots and ends.
Leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour, then rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
Use this mask once or twice a week to promote hair growth, strengthen hair follicles, and prevent hair loss.

2. Fenugreek Infused Oil:
Heat a carrier oil such as coconut oil or olive oil in a pan.
Add fenugreek seeds to the hot oil and let them simmer on low heat for a few minutes until they turn slightly brown.
Remove from heat and let the oil cool down.
Strain the oil to remove the fenugreek seeds.
Transfer the infused oil to a clean bottle or jar.
Massage the fenugreek-infused oil into your scalp and hair, ensuring thorough coverage.
Leave it on overnight or for a few hours, then shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
Regular use of fenugreek-infused oil helps nourish the scalp, strengthen hair roots, and promote shiny, healthy hair.

3. Fenugreek Water Rinse:
Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight.
Strain the seeds and collect the fenugreek-infused water.
Use this fenugreek water as a final rinse after shampooing your hair.
Gently massage the fenugreek water into your scalp and hair, focusing on the roots.
Leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with plain water.
Fenugreek water helps condition the hair, reduce dandruff, and promote hair growth

4. Fenugreek Hair Paste for Dandruff:
Grind fenugreek seeds into a fine powder.
Mix the fenugreek powder with water to form a thick paste.
Apply the paste to your scalp and massage it gently.
Leave it on for about 30 minutes, then rinse it out with lukewarm water.
Use this paste regularly to reduce dandruff, soothe scalp irritation, and maintain scalp health.
