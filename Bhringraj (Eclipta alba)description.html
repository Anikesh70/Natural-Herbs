Bhringraj (Eclipta alba) Description

1. Bhringraj Hair Oil:
Prepare Bhringraj hair oil by infusing Bhringraj leaves or powder in a carrier oil such as coconut oil or sesame oil.
Heat the carrier oil and add dried Bhringraj leaves or powder to it.
Let the mixture simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes.
Allow the oil to cool, strain out the leaves or powder, and store it in a clean, airtight container.
Massage this oil into your scalp and hair regularly, leave it on for a few hours or overnight, and then wash it off with a mild shampoo. Regular use can help nourish the scalp, strengthen hair follicles, and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Bhringraj Hair Mask:
Prepare a hair mask by mixing Bhringraj powder with yogurt or aloe vera gel to form a smooth paste.
Apply this paste to your scalp and hair, covering all strands from roots to tips.
Leave the mask on for about 30 minutes to an hour.
Rinse it off thoroughly with lukewarm water and shampoo.
This hair mask helps in conditioning the hair, promoting hair growth, and preventing hair fall

3. Bhringraj Shampoo:
You can either purchase Bhringraj-based shampoos available in the market or make your own by adding Bhringraj extract or powder to your regular shampoo.
Mix a small amount of Bhringraj extract or powder with your shampoo just before using it.
Wash your hair with this shampoo as usual.
Bhringraj shampoo helps in cleansing the scalp, stimulating hair follicles, and improving hair texture.

4. Bhringraj Hair Rinse:
Prepare a Bhringraj hair rinse by boiling Bhringraj leaves or powder in water for 10-15 minutes.
Allow the mixture to cool and strain it.
Use this infusion as a final hair rinse after shampooing and conditioning your hair.
It helps in conditioning the hair, adding shine, and promoting hair growth.

5. Bhringraj Capsules or Supplements:
Bhringraj supplements or capsules are also available in the market, which can be consumed orally.
Follow the dosage instructions provided on the product packaging or consult a healthcare professional for guidance.
Consuming Bhringraj supplements regularly can help in promoting overall hair health from within.
