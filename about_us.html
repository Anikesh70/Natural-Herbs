Healthy Food

Healthy food is like fuel for our bodies. Just like a car needs good fuel to run smoothly, our bodies need nutritious food to function properly. When we eat healthy foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins, we give our bodies the vitamins, minerals, and energy they need to keep us going strong. Healthy food helps our bodies grow, repair tissues, and fight off illnesses. It gives us the energy to do all the things we love, like playing sports, studying, or spending time with friends. Plus, when we eat well, we feel better both physically and mentally.
