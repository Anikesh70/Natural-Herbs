Symptoms of Fever

Fever symptoms include feeling hot, shivering, headache, muscle aches, sweating, and general weakness. It is the body's response to infections, helping to fight off harmful pathogens by raising its temperature.

Homemade Herb Remedies

  • Tulsi (Holy Basil) Tea:

    Boil a few tulsi leaves in water and simmer for a few minutes. Strain the liquid and drink it warm. Tulsi helps reduce fever and boosts immunity.

  • Neem Water:

    Boil neem leaves in water, strain, and consume the water lukewarm. Neem has antipyretic properties that help reduce fever and fight infections.

  • Ginger and Turmeric Decoction:

    Boil ginger and turmeric in water, strain, and drink the decoction. Ginger and turmeric have anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties that help reduce fever and alleviate symptoms.

  • Coriander Water:

    Boil coriander seeds in water, strain, and drink the water warm. Coriander seeds have cooling properties that can help lower body temperature during fever.

  • Fenugreek Seed Tea:

    Boil fenugreek seeds in water, strain, and drink the tea warm. Fenugreek seeds induce sweating, which helps reduce fever by cooling the body.

  • Cinnamon and Black Pepper Tea:

    Boil cinnamon and black pepper in water, strain, and drink the tea warm. Both spices have antipyretic properties that help reduce fever and fight infections.

  • Cardamom and Clove Infusion:

    Boil cardamom and clove in water, strain, and drink the infusion warm. Cardamom has cooling properties, while clove has antimicrobial properties.

  • Cumin Seeds Tea:

    Boil cumin seeds in water and strain the liquid. Drink this cumin tea to induce sweating and lower body temperature during fever.
