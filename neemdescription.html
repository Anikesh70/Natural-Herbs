Neem Description

1. Neem Leaves: Neem leaves can be consumed orally or used topically in various forms:
a) Oral Consumption: Neem leaves can be chewed directly or brewed into a tea. Neem tea is believed to have detoxifying properties and is used to treat conditions such as gastrointestinal disorders, diabetes, and fever.
b) Topical Application: Crushed neem leaves or neem leaf paste can be applied directly to the skin to treat skin conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, and fungal infections. Neem leaf paste can also be used as a poultice to treat wounds, cuts, and burns.

2. Neem Oil: Neem oil is extracted from the seeds of the neem tree and is known for its potent antifungal, antibacterial, and antiviral properties. It can be used topically to treat various skin conditions and ailments:
a) Skin Conditions: Apply neem oil directly to the affected area to treat acne, eczema, psoriasis, and fungal infections like athlete's foot and ringworm.
b) Scalp Health: Neem oil can be massaged into the scalp to treat dandruff, scalp psoriasis, and other scalp infections. It also helps strengthen hair follicles and promote healthy hair growth.

3. Neem Capsules or Supplements: Neem supplements in the form of capsules or tablets are available and can be consumed orally to support overall health and boost immunity. Neem supplements are believed to have antiparasitic, antiviral, and immune-enhancing properties.

4. Neem Bark: Neem bark is also used in traditional medicine and can be consumed as a tea to treat gastrointestinal issues such as ulcers, indigestion, and intestinal worms.

5. Neem Twigs for Oral Hygiene: Chewing on neem twigs (also known as neem datun) is a traditional practice in India for maintaining oral hygiene. Neem twigs have antibacterial properties and are believed to help prevent dental caries, gum disease, and bad breath.

6. Neem Extracts in Cosmetics: Neem extracts are commonly used in cosmetic products such as soaps, shampoos, lotions, and creams due to their antibacterial and skin-nourishing properties
